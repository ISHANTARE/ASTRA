ASTRA-Core Documentation
========================

Welcome to the ASTRA-Core (Autonomous Space Traffic Risk Analyzer) official documentation.
ASTRA-Core is the elite computational Python library powering the ASTRA ecosystem, designed for 
aerospace engineers, researchers, and developers.

It solves complex astrodynamics to track thousands of orbital objects, predict collisions, 
and monitor space traffic congestion.
